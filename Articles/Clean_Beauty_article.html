Best Clean Products For The Winter

Beauticians say these are a must for surviving the winter.

By Ai Paige February 27, 2025 6 min read

Winter can be brutal on the skin. The cold, dry air strips away moisture, leaving your face feeling parched and your lips cracked. But don't worry—I've got your back. These five clean beauty products are my go-to picks for the season. Not only do they hydrate, protect, and nourish, but they’re free from harmful chemicals, so you can feel good about what you’re putting on your skin. Here’s why they’re my winter favorites:

1. True Botanicals Nourishing Oil

This luxurious oil has been a game-changer for me. During winter, my skin craves hydration, and this nourishing oil delivers it in spades. Packed with a blend of organic, nutrient-rich oils like chia seed, rosehip, and passionfruit, it deeply hydrates without feeling greasy. The lightweight formula sinks in quickly, leaving my skin plump and glowing. It’s perfect for dry patches and keeping your skin nourished all day long.

2. Ilia Beauty Super Serum Skin Tint

When the weather gets colder, I find that my skin needs something a little more protective, and this tinted serum is a must-have. It combines SPF 40 with a skin-boosting blend of antioxidants and hyaluronic acid, so it hydrates while offering protection from the harsh winter sun. The dewy finish gives you that healthy, radiant glow, while the buildable coverage evens out skin tone without feeling heavy. It's the perfect everyday product for glowing, healthy skin in the winter months.

3. Herbivore Botanicals Cloud Jelly Hydrating Gel

For those days when your skin feels like it’s literally craving a drink, this hydrating gel is an absolute lifesaver. With a mix of hyaluronic acid, aloe vera, and tremella mushroom, it plumps up and replenishes your skin. It’s so light and refreshing, and you can layer it under your moisturizer for that extra boost of hydration. This clean beauty gem leaves my skin feeling soft and hydrated without any stickiness, making it perfect for those colder months when my skin is desperate for moisture.

4. Tata Harper Resurfacing Mask

Exfoliation is key during winter, as dry air can lead to flaky skin and clogged pores. This mask is gentle yet effective, thanks to its blend of natural AHAs, BHAs, and fruit enzymes. It sloughs off dead skin cells, leaving my skin smoother, brighter, and more even-toned. I love that it's 100% natural and made with clean ingredients, so I know I'm treating my skin to something good. Plus, the slight tingle lets you know it's working its magic!

5. RMS Beauty Lip2Cheek

Winter often brings dry lips, so I need something that will hydrate and give me a pop of color. This multitasker is a must-have for lips and cheeks. Made with coconut oil and shea butter, it’s ultra-hydrating, and the clean pigments give you a soft, natural flush. The formula blends beautifully, and I love how it stays on all day. Whether you’re out running errands or going out for a cozy dinner, this product keeps your lips and cheeks hydrated and glowing.

Why These Products?

Winter can be harsh on your skin, so choosing clean, nourishing products is essential to keeping it healthy and glowing. These five picks not only provide hydration and protection but are also free from parabens, sulfates, and artificial fragrances—so you can trust that they’ll treat your skin with care. Plus, they’re all made with ingredients that help lock in moisture and give your skin a healthy, dewy glow. Your winter beauty routine just got a lot easier (and cleaner) with these products!
